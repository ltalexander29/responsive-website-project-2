Welcome Hunters!

Monster Hunter is a series where players hunt massive monsters, craft weapons and armor, and explore large environments.

Why I Like Monster Hunter

The combat is deep, every weapon feels unique, and the monster designs are incredible.

The Combat

The combat is the mian draw of Monster Hunter. There are 14 unique weapon types, each with a VASTLY different play styles. Each also has individual strengths and weaknesses. I'm particular to the charge blade myslef.

The Monsters

In a game called Monster Hunter, as you can imagine, the monsters are fantastic. Not only do they have a variety of visual designs, but each monster behaves, fights, and reacts, in unique ways. Each monster feels like a true and genuine monster.

The Exploration

The world these monsters live in is fantastic. Finding the monsters is half the fun as you get to explore a variety of genuinely beautiful environments. Deserts, Forests, Volcanos, Coral Forests, and all sorts of varied worlds to explore.
